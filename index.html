About

We are a nonprofit association of member states that promotes excellence in the field of skills and competency development. We raise awareness of the importance of professional excellence and high-quality vocational education and training for Asian youth and economies.

We work in four main areas:

Skill competitions and WorldSkills Asia Event

WorldSkills Asia is a skills competition where the best of Asian national skills competitions will compete every two years. It will showcase and inspire world-class excellence in skills and introduce young people to a variety of skilled careers around Asia.

Promoting skills

We are a platform where educators, policy-makers and industry can work to redefine the value and improve the attractiveness of skills. We encourage the transfer of skills and knowledge, as well as cultural exchange between young people around the region.

International cooperation and development

We are developing strategic partnerships with corporate, government, and non-government entities, to develop training and skills across the region.

Education and training

We will be the hub for Asian skills networks, information and knowledge developing new opportunities for skills development and innovation.
